What is the best music to code by when working on open source projects?

We get the question all the time. You really need something that will allow you to get into the state of flow. Something that is not too distracting, but can block out extraneous noise. Surprisingly, many swear by video game soundtracks. They are often engineered for just that purpose. And some of them have extraordinary music that will honestly help you focus rather than distract you.
